## Verifiable predictions

- Draymond predicts Steve Kerr will NOT return as Warriors head coach next season
  * **Feeling it was the end:** The farewell moment shared between Draymond, Steph, and Steve after the play-in loss felt final — Draymond says "it just felt like that was it"
  * **Surrounding talk:** All the rumours and discourse circulating about Steve's future pointed him toward not coming back

- Draymond predicts Jimmy Butler will miss a considerable portion of next season recovering from surgery
  * **Injury timeline:** Jimmy got hurt in January, had surgery roughly 6 weeks later; a 12-month recovery puts his return around late February or March of next season
  * **Ramp-up needed:** Even after returning from surgery, Draymond notes Jimmy would still need ramp-up time and time to get past the fear of contact

- Draymond predicts Moses will miss a good portion of next season due to injury
  * **Mentioned alongside Jimmy:** Grouped him directly with Butler as players who "will probably be out for a good amount next year" when assessing the Warriors' near-term outlook

- Draymond predicts he has approximately 3 more years remaining in his NBA career
  * **Physical condition:** Says his body felt great this season and he finished it healthy, which he views as a major positive
  * **Motivation to improve:** Wants to stay active this summer with minimal time off the court, believing he still has areas of his game to develop
