- Draymond Green predicts the Spurs and Thunder will face each other in the Western Conference Finals.
  * **Spurs' playoff readiness:** Victor Wembanyama, Stephon Castle, and De'Aaron Fox all look like seasoned playoff players rather than a young team seeing the postseason for the first time.
