## Verifiable predictions

- Draymond Green predicts the Detroit Pistons will win the first-round series against the Cleveland Cavaliers.
  * **Physicality advantage:** Draymond argues the Pistons are simply too physical for the Cavs, with Jared Allen and Evan Mobley both struggling against that physicality — Evan Mobley visibly stepping away from contact.
  * **Size/athleticism mismatch:** Detroit's lineup (Duren at 7 ft, Ausar Thompson at 6'8", Tobias Harris at 6'8") presents an overwhelming size and athleticism problem for Cleveland's guards and bigs.

  * **Series dominance:** The Knicks lead 3-0 after a 108-94 Game 3 win; Draymond says the Sixers will not come back from 3-0 as they did from 3-1 the prior series.
  * **Rhythm and balance:** New York is "hotter than any hottest fish grease right now," with Jalen Brunson (33 pts), Mikal Bridges (23 pts), and Josh Hart (12 pts/11 reb) all contributing.
