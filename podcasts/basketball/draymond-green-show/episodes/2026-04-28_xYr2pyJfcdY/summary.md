## Summary

Draymond goes series-by-series through the first round of the 2026 NBA playoffs, giving
his read on each matchup and who advances. He opens on OKC's dominance over Phoenix and a
long riff on officiating accountability (the "Gucci"/James Williams complaints, Shai's
foul-drawing). He covers Minnesota's injuries (Ant out, DiVincenzo's torn Achilles) but
still backs Denver to come back, breaks down Detroit-Orlando, Lakers-Rockets (KD hurt),
Spurs-Blazers, Cavs-Raptors (urging Brandon Ingram to stay aggressive), Knicks-Hawks
(pushing Atlanta to play Buddy Hield, praising JK), and Celtics-Sixers (skeptical of
re-inserting Embiid into the starting lineup). He also rips the Trail Blazers' new owner
for cost-cutting and lists arena standards he thinks the NBA should mandate.
